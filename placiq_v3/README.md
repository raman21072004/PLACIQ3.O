PlaCIQ 3.0 — Agentic AI Placement Intelligence System
HackAI Season 2 Submission

PlaCIQ 3.0 is an advanced multi-agent AI platform designed to transform the student placement journey through autonomous intelligence, real-time market awareness, and personalized career guidance.

It acts as an AI-powered placement mentor that helps students understand where they stand, what skills they need, and how to systematically improve their chances of landing top jobs.

🚀 Overview

In today’s competitive hiring landscape, students often struggle with:

Lack of personalized career guidance
Unclear job market trends
Weak interview preparation
Poor ATS resume optimization
No structured placement roadmap

PlaCIQ 3.0 solves these problems by combining five intelligent AI agents into one seamless ecosystem.

🤖 Core Idea

Instead of offering generic advice, PlaCIQ 3.0 behaves like a team of AI career experts working together:

Analyze student profile
Detect skill gaps
Fetch live industry demand insights
Build custom placement strategy
Prepare students for interviews
Optimize resumes for recruiters & ATS systems

Resume
Skills
